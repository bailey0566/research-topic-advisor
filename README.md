# Research Topic Builder for Master's Students

An interactive web application designed to guide master's students in education through the process of selecting and refining research topics for their theses or dissertations.

## Features

- **Topic Selection**: Browse through categorized educational research topics
- **Role Definition**: Choose or customize your research role and context
- **Problem Statement Development**: Craft clear, researchable problem statements with guided scaffolding
- **Feasibility Assessment**: Evaluate research feasibility using Likert-scale surveys across multiple dimensions
- **Measurement Planning**: Define multiple ways to measure your research outcomes
- **AI Integration**: Generate prompts for Gemini AI to help refine your research approach
- **Export Options**: Export your work in multiple formats (print, email, etc.)
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress indicator and local data persistence

## How to Use

1. **Select a Topic**: Choose from categorized educational research areas
2. **Define Your Role**: Select your position or add a custom role
3. **Craft Your Problem**: Use guided prompts to develop a clear research problem
4. **Assess Feasibility**: Rate your research across access, scope, clarity, and logistics dimensions
5. **Plan Measurements**: Define how you'll measure the impact of your research
6. **Get AI Help**: Generate prompts for Gemini AI to refine your approach
7. **Export Your Work**: Save or share your research plan

## Technology Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3
- Vanilla JavaScript
- jsPDF (for PDF export)

## Local Development

1. Clone the repository
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. No build process required - it's a static web application
